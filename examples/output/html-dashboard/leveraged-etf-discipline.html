Generic 2x Equity Index ETF

Ledger ID: leveraged-etf-discipline. Updated: 2026-05-12.

Brief

Leveraged ETFs can express short-term tactical views, but path dependency, volatility drag, and position sizing rules must dominate the decision process.

Assumptions

  • A1: The instrument is used for a defined short-term thesis rather than a long-term allocation. (confidence: high; sources: S1)
  • A2: Volatility conditions are reviewed before entry and daily while open. (confidence: medium; sources: S2)

Catalysts

  • CAT2: Macro event calendar supports a time-boxed tactical setup. on 2026-06-10 (status: required; sources: S1)
  • CAT1: Index breaks above predefined technical level. during before entry (status: required; sources: none)

Positions

  • Maximum planned holding period is five trading days.
  • Position size must be smaller than the equivalent unlevered exposure.
